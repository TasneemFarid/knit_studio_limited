Everybody talks about sustainability.
We’re here to help you understand more about it.

Our source is Mother Earth

We pay the highest attention in the selection of raw materials, the use of recycled materials, the reduction of environmental impact and the safety of our teams. These are the eight international certifications that demonstrate our willingness and commitment.

But Why?!

We firmly believe that without a healthy planet and without the dignity of the people who live on it, there is no possibility for the future, nor for development. That is why our commitment does not end with achieving certifications.

People

The people who make our fabrics are at the very centre of our supply chain. We strive to ensure that every member of the team has the opportunity to be trained and to grow professionally. In a healthy and safe environment.

Energy efficiency

We keep looking for new ways to improve water and energy savings. In finishing and dyeing we have implemented sophisticated sensors and control devices, which enable us to save around 30 million litres of water per year.

Thermal saving

Making the most out of resources is essential to reducing our impact. We are committed to optimizing the use of the thermal energy we produce by investing in machines that recover heat, enabling us to produce more while consuming less.

Water conservation

We have acted on production methods to achieve water savings of around 8%. In 2020, we invested in the development of state-of-the-art technology to reduce water consumption in the dying process.
