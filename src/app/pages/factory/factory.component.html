A 100% export-oriented sweater manufacturer, delivers premium, sustainable knitwear to global brands, combining innovation, quality, and eco-friendly practices to ensure style, comfort, and timely delivery.

Mission

Engage effectively, responsibly and profitably in the global textile industry.

To become a brand that is known for its distinctive and uncompromising quality. Knit Studio Ltd is promised to produce world-class sweaters for men, women, children, and kids. We strive to ensure our customer satisfaction with our product quality and on-time shipment schedule.

Vision

To create a single channel for satisfying diverse and high class needs of our value partners.

To become a window through which all our interacting parties can see and feel their prospects and dream about their success. We perform in unison by following our values and ethical guidelines, using our code of conduct as a source of guidance and inspiration, enabling us to achieve our vision.
